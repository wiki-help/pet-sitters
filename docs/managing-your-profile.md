# Managing Your FurKid Profile

Your FurKid profile is the foundation of great pet care. A complete, accurate profile helps sitters provide personalized care and ensures nothing important gets missed while you're away.

## Pet Profiles

### Adding a New Pet

For each pet you add, include:

- **Name and Photo** — Helps the sitter bond with your pet right away
- **Breed, Age, and Weight** — Critical for medication dosing, exercise needs, and diet
- **Sex and Spay/Neuter Status** — Relevant for behavior and health considerations
- **Color and Markings** — Helps with identification and走失情况

### Health Information

Be thorough and honest:

- **Medications** — Name, dosage, schedule, and administration method (pill, liquid, injection)
- **Allergies** — Food, environmental, and medication allergies
- **Medical Conditions** — Diabetes, arthritis, anxiety, heart conditions, etc.
- **Diet** — Food brand, portion size, feeding times, and treat restrictions
- **Vet Info** — Primary vet name, phone, address, and preferred emergency clinic

### Behavior and Routine

The more detail, the better the care:

- **Temperament** — Friendly, shy, anxious, reactive, high energy, laid back
- **Socialization** — Good with dogs, cats, kids, strangers? Any triggers?
- **Exercise Needs** — Walk length, frequency, off-leash rules, favorite routes
- **Sleep Habits** — Where they sleep, bedtime routine, any night wakings
- **Hiding Spots** — Where they retreat when scared — so the sitter doesn't panic if your pet disappears
- **Favorite Toys and Games** — Helps the sitter bond and keep your pet happy
- **House Rules** — Furniture allowed? Counter surfing? Crate trained? Room restrictions?

### Emergency Contacts

Add at least two contacts:

- **Primary Contact** — You, with phone and email
- **Local Backup** — A neighbor, friend, or family member who can help in person
- **Vet Contact** — Your regular veterinarian
- **Emergency Vet** — 24-hour clinic closest to your home

## Your Account Settings

### Contact Information

Keep your email, phone number, and address current. Sitters may need to reach you during a stay.

### Payment Methods

Add a credit or debit card for secure payments. FurKid uses industry-standard encryption. You can:

- Add multiple payment methods
- Set a default card
- View your billing history
- Update or remove cards anytime

### Notification Preferences

Choose how you want to hear from FurKid:

- **Booking Confirmations** — Email and/or SMS
- **Sitter Messages** — Real-time notifications
- **Photo Updates** — Push or SMS alerts
- **Promotions** — Optional deals and feature announcements

### Subscription Plans

FurKid offers optional memberships for frequent pet parents:

- **Pay Per Visit** — Free to join. Pay only when you book. No monthly fees.
- **FurKid Plus** — $15/month (annual). 5% off every booking, priority sitter access, unlimited meet-and-greets, and 24/7 support.
- **FurKid Family** — $29/month (annual). 10% off every booking, same-day booking guarantee, and a dedicated account manager.

You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your account settings.

## Privacy and Security

### Data Protection

FurKid encrypts all personal information and payment details. We never sell your data to third parties. Review our [Privacy Policy](https://fur-kid.com/privacy-policy/) for full details.

### Profile Visibility

- Your **pet profile details** are shared only with sitters you book or request a meet-and-greet with
- Your **reviews** are public and help other pet parents
- Your **contact information** is not visible to other pet parents

## Keeping Your Profile Updated

Life changes — and your profile should reflect that:

- **New Medication?** Update immediately before the next booking
- **Diet Change?** Adjust feeding instructions before the sitter arrives
- **Behavior Shift?** Note any new anxiety, aggression, or preferences
- **New Pet?** Add them before searching for a sitter
- **Moved?** Update your address so sitters can find you
