# Getting Started with FurKid

Welcome! This guide will help you create your account, set up your profile, and book your first pet sitter — or launch your sitter business — in just a few minutes.

## For Pet Parents

### 1. Create Your Account

Sign up at [fur-kid.com](https://fur-kid.com) with your email or Google account. The process takes under two minutes.

### 2. Add Your Pets

Build a profile for each pet you need care for:

- **Basic Info** — Name, breed, age, weight, and photo
- **Health & Diet** — Medications, allergies, feeding schedule, and dietary restrictions
- **Behavior** — Temperament, socialization with other pets/kids, anxiety triggers
- **Routine** — Walk schedule, sleep habits, favorite toys, and hiding spots
- **Emergency Contacts** — Vet info, preferred emergency clinic, and a local backup contact

### 3. Search for Sitters

Enter your ZIP code and browse vetted local sitters. Filter by:

- Service type (dog walking, drop-in visits, overnight sitting, house sitting, day care)
- Availability and calendar
- Experience level and certifications
- Pet types they specialize in
- Price range

### 4. Schedule a Meet-and-Greet

Before booking, request a free meet-and-greet. This no-obligation introduction lets you:

- Observe how the sitter interacts with your pet
- Discuss your pet's routine and special needs
- Ask about emergency protocols and backup plans
- Confirm logistics like keys, alarm codes, and parking

### 5. Book and Pay Securely

Confirm your booking through FurKid's secure platform. You pay the sitter's rate — FurKid adds no platform fees. You'll receive instant confirmation and can message your sitter anytime before the stay.

## For Sitters

### 1. Apply to Become a Sitter

Register at [fur-kid.com/sitter/register](https://fur-kid.com/sitter/register). Provide:

- Your name, email, and ZIP code
- Services you offer
- Years of pet care experience

### 2. Pass the Background Check

FurKid requires a background check through our partner Checkr. You'll receive an email with instructions after submitting your application. Most checks clear within 1–3 business days.

### 3. Build Your Profile

A complete profile builds trust and gets you booked faster:

- **Professional Photo** — A friendly, clear headshot
- **Bio** — Highlight your experience, specialties, and love for pets
- **Services & Rates** — Set competitive prices for each service you offer
- **Availability** — Keep your calendar up to date
- **Photos** — Share images of pets you've cared for (with owner permission)

### 4. Set Your Rates

You keep 100% of your rate — FurKid takes zero commission. Research local rates and price yourself based on your experience, services, and demand in your area.

### 5. Start Accepting Bookings

Once activated, your profile appears in local search results. Respond quickly to booking requests and messages to build a strong reputation and earn great reviews.
