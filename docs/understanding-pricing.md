# Understanding FurKid Pricing

FurKid's pricing is built on one simple principle: **transparency**. No hidden fees, no surprise charges. Here's exactly how it works.

## The Zero-Fee Model

Unlike other platforms, FurKid charges **no service fees** on pay-per-visit bookings. Here's what that means:

| | FurKid | Other Platforms |
|---|---|---|
| What pet parent pays | Sitter's stated rate only | Sitter's rate + 20–30% service fee |
| What sitter receives | Full rate minus payment processing | Rate minus platform commission |
| Total cost for a $50 booking | **$50** | $60–$65 |

This saves pet parents money and helps sitters win more bookings.

## Pay-Per-Visit Pricing

### For Pet Parents

You pay only what the sitter charges. Period.

- **Dog Walking** — Typically $15–$30 per walk, depending on duration and location
- **Drop-In Visits** — Usually $15–$25 per visit for feeding, play, and basic care
- **Overnight Sitting** — Generally $40–$85 per night, depending on the number of pets
- **House Sitting** — Similar to overnight rates, often with additional pet care included
- **Day Care** — Typically $25–$50 per day

Sitters set their own rates based on experience, demand, and location. You can filter search results by price range.

### For Sitters

You keep your full stated rate, minus standard payment processing fees (typically 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction, charged by our payment processor — not by FurKid).

Example on a $50 booking:
- Pet parent pays: $50.00
- Payment processing fee: ~$1.75
- You receive: ~$48.25

FurKid takes nothing.

## Cancellations and Refunds

### Pet Parent Cancellations

Each sitter sets their own cancellation policy. Common structures:

- **Flexible** — Full refund if cancelled 24+ hours before the stay
- **Moderate** — 50% refund if cancelled 48+ hours before; no refund within 48 hours
- **Strict** — No refund if cancelled within 7 days of the stay

The sitter's policy is displayed on their profile before you book. FurKid enforces the stated policy.

### Sitter Cancellations

If a sitter cancels a confirmed booking, the pet parent receives a full refund immediately. Sitters who cancel repeatedly may be removed from the platform.

### Subscription Cancellations

You can cancel Plus or Family anytime. Your membership remains active until the end of the current billing period. We do not offer prorated refunds.

## Tipping

Tipping is optional but appreciated:

- **Standard tip** — 10–15% for good service
- **Generous tip** — 20% for exceptional care, last-minute availability, or extra services
- **No tip expected** — Sitters price their services to reflect their full rate

Tips are processed through the FurKid platform and go directly to the sitter.

## Payment Processing

### Security

All payments are processed through PCI-compliant payment infrastructure. FurKid never stores your full credit card number on our servers.

### Payout Schedule for Sitters

Sitters choose their payout schedule:

- **Instant** — Available for a small fee; funds arrive within minutes
- **Standard** — Free; funds arrive within 2–3 business days

### Failed Payments

If a pet parent's payment fails, the booking is not confirmed. The pet parent is notified to update their payment method. Sitters are not expected to begin service until payment clears.

## Taxes

### For Pet Parents

Pet care expenses may be tax-deductible in certain situations (e.g., if you run a business from home and the pet care enables work). Consult a tax professional.

### For Sitters

You are an independent contractor. You are responsible for:

- Reporting all income
- Paying self-employment tax
- Making quarterly estimated payments if needed
- Tracking deductible expenses

FurKid does not withhold taxes. You will receive a 1099 form if you earn above the reporting threshold.
