# How FurKid Works

FurKid is a pet care marketplace that connects pet parents with trusted local sitters and dog walkers. Our zero-fee model means you pay less — and sitters earn more.

## What Is FurKid?

FurKid is an online platform where pet parents can find, vet, and book independent pet caregivers in their neighborhood. We handle identity verification, background checks, secure payments, and review systems so both sides can book with confidence.

We do not employ sitters directly. Every caregiver on FurKid is an independent contractor who sets their own rates, chooses their own schedule, and manages their own business.

## Key Features

### For Pet Parents

- **Verified Sitters** — Every sitter passes an identity verification and background check before going live
- **Detailed Profiles** — Browse experience, reviews, photos, services, and rates
- **Free Meet-and-Greets** — Meet your sitter before committing to a booking
- **Secure Payments** — Pay through the platform with full booking protection
- **Live Updates** — Receive photo updates and walk reports during the stay
- **Zero Platform Fees** — You pay only the sitter's rate on pay-per-visit bookings

### For Sitters

- **Keep 100% of Your Rate** — FurKid charges zero commission on bookings
- **Set Your Own Prices** — Control what you charge for each service
- **Flexible Schedule** — Accept bookings that fit your availability
- **Direct Payouts** — Earnings deposited straight to your bank account
- **Review System** — Build a reputation that attracts more pet parents
- **Secure Platform** — All bookings and payments handled safely through FurKid

## The Zero-Fee Difference

Most pet care platforms charge pet parents a service fee and take a commission from the sitter — inflating the total cost by 20–30%. At FurKid:

- **Pet parents** pay only the sitter's stated rate
- **Sitters** keep their full earnings minus standard payment processing costs
- **Everyone saves** — lower costs mean more families can afford care, and sitters get more bookings

## Safety and Trust

### Background Checks

Every sitter must pass a background check through Checkr before they can accept bookings. We verify identity and check criminal history.

### Reviews

After every booking, pet parents leave verified reviews. These reviews are tied to actual bookings and cannot be faked or removed by the sitter.

### Booking Protection

Every booking made through FurKid includes:

- Secure payment processing
- Cancellation policy enforcement
- 24/7 support for urgent issues
- Dispute resolution assistance

### Communication

All initial messaging happens through the FurKid platform. We recommend keeping communication on-platform until you've established trust with your sitter.
